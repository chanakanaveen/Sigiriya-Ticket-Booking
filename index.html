    Welcome to Sigiriya

    Sigiriya is one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic UNESCO World Heritage Sites, attracting visitors from around the globe with its majestic rock palace, ancient frescoes, and breathtaking views. With increasing popularity comes the need for responsible tourism and effective visitor management.

    Our mission is to preserve the heritage of Sigiriya while enhancing the experience for every visitor. This website has been designed to manage visitor flow through a convenient time-slot booking system, reduce overcrowding, and ensure your visit is smooth, enjoyable, and safe.
